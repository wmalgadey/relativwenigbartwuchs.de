## Easily Face Swap Midjourney Images

InsightFace’s ability to face swap has blown us away with it’s ability to match expressions, angles, lighting, & styles.



![](https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/d5488f26-5115-44a1-a7e3-9b277fdb3143/FaceSwapMidjourneyImagesWithTwoClicksFreeonline-video-cutter.com-ezgif.com-optimize.gif?t=1704940367)

### Use Cases

- Make your own stock photos

- Create consistent characters

- Enhance a virtual try-on experience

- Personalize product demos

- Face swap yourself or celebrities for fun (e.g. alter your family photos or customize virtual greeting cards)



### Steps

1. **Create your own Discord server** (_we recommend this for anyone using Midjourney_)

- Click the ‘+’ button in the bottom left of Discord

- Select ‘Create My Own’

- Select ‘For me and my friends’

- Name your server & click ‘Create’

2. **Invite the Midjourney Bot to your server**

- Click on the ‘Midjourney Bot’ on the right-side panel

- Click ‘Add to Server’

- From the ‘Add to Server’ dropdown menu, select your server

- Click ‘Continue’ then ‘Authorize’

3. **Invite Insight Face Swap Bot to your server**

- Click on [this link](https://link.mail.beehiiv.com/ss/c/RHRRIjeJp99X0xx5bp2QFxZb6Gtk48magqvJ8E3eKcEuRT8yUCtHde4P50uOSZZ-hV2KDeQYFB_HLM1-RPWD1Ikw5ryq4l2TfDtK4hhqSmzgPziUHtMg9AHFpd8mMc1NXTT8Fnbh0EblUbQGIbeemEwdZVSssXJPVPqiunLvSgTYgE5BEYjJ4Hi2Mc9IclfgYCuxrxQW05gzVt_ExuYl6Ekwi4aNu9Yu1eHWkmj0jerWyLbuWD57_1MrVVThR10uSnH-Zq4YwUJAVTOYyEyBZRJjqENkQ8jUseJ6pi1DUKU4w1ww0qMwk7VvVh2SLmijEM9R8Vnq3HMynhPySsJzQzyQW_JcKt2cIhqD3Sk6PpxPBds4t9Umjn9df3sW1r7MjlikwF6rcma-GS99r1tmaGaHGh4_DiQUNo43sz_s539Xv08dZFHl5FrcOWZHjvbiZnY1zE53FVNr0YK9cmxg36RIkghZV513j1qNbXZ26KdhkK5M1QCA16Rtwq2ekiXZWGENq7EVkITwtcOSoHOM3iBdeLHstcyb_SNzImC1x2_xeU8iD4HTTQS0GXreODHUapq7neK2Y2LKAzUFGwq9QbkSqSI6R-LwLrPjjoyXKryDTtPyZ41tYVh_5gE-6Fzl54EJhMgMyOKzkpx4W7Mz5eK4K6TkucLhDYRHalLtUVTEyZ7vTudlV4AKpxlnGKcp5IzgrrEy0EXGCgdOrZENhw/42w/iIcyVOYuSW675-1euBgeWg/h38/ZjdZqJhVaYbu3449nI5-SygUAtXZdMnfqgHsyLpRTiA) (or send the link as a message to your server)

- From the ‘Add to Server’ dropdown menu, select your server

- Click ‘Continue’ then ‘Authorize’

4. **Upload the face you want to swap**

- Use the command: /saveid & hit enter

- Drag and drop the face you want to save (ideally high-quality, forward-facing, & with no hair or glasses covering the face)

- Type a name & hit enter

5. **Find an image to swap your uploaded face with**

- Type your prompt (we used “photo of a man, portrait”)

- Select U1, U2, U3, or U4 to upscale the option you like

6. **Swap the faces**

- Right click on the image

- Hover over ‘Apps’

- Select ‘INSwapper’
