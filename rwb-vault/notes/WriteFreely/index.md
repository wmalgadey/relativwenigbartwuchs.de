Our fast, auto-saving editor is all you need to quickly get your thoughts down and published to your blog.

WriteFreely sets your ideas and your server's resources free. Just run the binary to start your site up.

WriteFreely has spent the past six years reliably powering more than 150,000 blogs on [Write.as](https://write.as/).

## Create a minimalist blog

WriteFreely is built around writing. There's no news feed, notifications, or unnecessary _likes_ or _claps_ to take you away from your train of thought. You get a [distraction-free writing environment](https://write.as/new), and readers can enjoy a [clean reading experience](https://blog.writefreely.org/).

## Publish to the fediverse

Reach outside your own site with federation via ActivityPub. WriteFreely lets anyone on [Mastodon](https://joinmastodon.org/), Pleroma, or any ActivityPub-enabled service follow your blog, bookmark your posts, and share them with their followers. [See how it works](https://video.writeas.org/videos/watch/cc55e615-d204-417c-9575-7b57674cc6f3).

## Easy on the eyes (and your server)

WriteFreely is written in Go (golang), so it runs anywhere and takes up very few resources — you can even run it on a Raspberry Pi! Installation is as easy as downloading a binary and a few supporting files, no other dependencies required.
