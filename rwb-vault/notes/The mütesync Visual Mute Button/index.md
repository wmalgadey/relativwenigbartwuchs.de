It’s super annoying when you don’t know if you’re muted on a Zoom call. Or when you lose your Google Meet tab and someone calls your name. That’s why we built a virtual and physical lighted mute button for Zoom. If the mütesync menu icon is red or the physical button light is glowing red, your mic is live!

And it’s more than a simple mute toggle! The mütesync app stays in sync with all your favorite video conferencing apps like Zoom and Google Meet. Even if the host mutes you, or you mute in the Zoom or Meet app, the virtual and physical buttons light up with the correct mute status!
