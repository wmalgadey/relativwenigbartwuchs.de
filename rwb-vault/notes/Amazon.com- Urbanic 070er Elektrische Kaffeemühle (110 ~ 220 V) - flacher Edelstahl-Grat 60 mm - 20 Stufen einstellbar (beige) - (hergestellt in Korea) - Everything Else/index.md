Reviewed in the United States on January 30, 2022

I've had this grinder for just over a month, and am using it on 220V. It also works on 110v (dual voltage). I have 0 complaints about it and would recommend it all but the most elite of coffee enthusiasts.

Form: The grinder looks great, especially since it matches my Icona Vintage. It's easy to clean and looks much more aesthetically pleasing than most electric burr grinders I've seen. The top portion is metal with a high quality anodized finish, the base and hopper are plastic.

Function: this grinder works better than my expectations. The hopper holds about 250g of beans, but I personally use single servings at a time. There is a divider for switching beans as well.

The grinder has a "push button" which allows you to leave it on and it grinds when you push the receptacle against the dispenser, which is very convenient vs. A traditional on/off switch.

It grinds quickly and quietly. I would say it is half as loud as the hand burr grinder I used before. It grinds 17g in about 3 seconds and 30g in about 5. Grind size is consistent given you first grind a gram or so to clear out the retained coffee.

The dispenser has a nice anti-static nozzle that keeps coffee from going everywhere or sticking to the nozzle. Retainment is surprisingly low, at maybe .1g.

Switching grind settings is easy (there are 20). I highly recommend that you run the grinder while going from coarse to fine settings in order to prevent coffee fragments from jamming or damaging the internals. 20 has an almost knife-chopped appearance, and 1 is very fine. My espresso maker couldn't get through 2 or 3, depending on the beans. It's not as finely adjustable as the $1000 grinders available these days, but for 95% of people it will be more than enough.

Shipping: the seller does NOT ship APO apparently, but it will not tell you that at checkout. After waiting almost a month (with no tracking number) I messaged them to find out it was stuck in shipping and they had not reached out to me.

Once I messaged them they replied quickly, and despite a slight language barrier they were able to make it right and ship to a different address for no additional charge. Had they not been so quick to reply and reship I would not be providing a 5 star review, but I was very happy with the final resolution.
