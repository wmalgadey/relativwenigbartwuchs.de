Logseq supports both Markdown and Org-mode, you can open an existing directory or creating a new one. Your data will be stored only on this device.

After you opened your directory, it will create three sub-directories in that directory:

- /journals - store your journal pages
- /pages - store the other pages
- /logseq - store configuration, custom.css, and some metadata.
