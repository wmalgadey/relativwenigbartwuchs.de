Dimensions & weight

16.2g

**Connector**  
USB Type Micro-B  

**Certification level**  
CC EALS+  

**Chips**  
ST31H320 (secure) + STM32F042  

**Material**  
Brushed stainless steel and plastic  

**Capacity**  
100mAh battery  

**Compatibility**  
64-bits desktop computer (Windows 8+, macOS 10.8+, Linux) excluding ARM Processors.  
  
Also compatible with Android 7+ smartphones. You will need an OTG kit to connect your Nano S to your desktop or smartphone
