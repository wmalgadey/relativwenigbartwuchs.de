# No Neutral Wire Required

ZBMINI-L is a 1-gang Zigbee smart switch module that can easily make your existing wall switch smart even when no neutral wire is in the junction box.

- A Zigbee bridge is needed and sold separately.

![[E4B88DE99C80E8A681E58AA8E79A84_16.jpg]]

## Safety First

The SONOFF team designed the product in strict accordance with the certification requirements and continuously optimized the details so that ZBMINI-L could successfully pass the rigorous tests of the major formal certification bodies. In addition to the basic certification, ZBMINI-L has also passed the tougher TÜV certification.

![[E4B88DE99C80E8A681E58AA8E79A84_8.jpg]]

## Protection Against Electric Shock

Our product provides adequate protection against accidental contact with live parts in all accessible positions of normal use. Ensure the user’s safety in installation and use.

![[E4B88DE99C80E8A681E58AA8E79A84_16-1.jpg]]

## Superior Quality and Reliability As Always

For example, in terms of conduction, the electricity extraction circuit has been optimized by technical means so that the large number of harmonics generated in its operating state is reduced or eliminated. The conduction data can still meet the standard under full power load, thus reducing the interference to the grid.

![[E4B88DE99C80E8A681E58AA8E79A84_51.jpg]]

The high-quality relay used in the product has undergone 40,000 switch life tests, and the temperature rise after the test meets the standard.

![[E4B88DE99C80E8A681E58AA8E79A84_20.jpg]]

## Make Existing 2-way or 3-way Control Smart—Only One ZBMINI-L

By installing ZBMINI-L in one of your existing two- or multi-way control junction boxes to make the connected light smart.

![[E4B88DE99C80E8A681E58AA8E79A84_55.jpg]]

## Works with More Zigbee Hubs

ZBMINI-L is an end-device developed according to the standard Zigbee 3.0 protocol. It supports being added to any Zigbee hub developed according to the standard protocol theoretically.

![[E4B88DE99C80E8A681E58AA8E79A84.png]]
