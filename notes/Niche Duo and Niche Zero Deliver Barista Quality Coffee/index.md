## Deliciously fresh coffee

Our single dosing grinders have been designed to maximise the freshness of your grind. Professional burrs and a patented, straight through design ensure virtually zero grind retention, helping deliver every bit of flavour from your beans.

## Exactly to your taste

The stepless grind dial on our grinders allow you to easily change grind consistency to optimise your brew method. It’s as simple as selecting your grind size, pouring in your pre-weighed beans and flicking the switch.
