## About Creodocs Ltd.

Creodocs Ltd. is a small business based in New Zealand, developed and operated by the founder, [Vel](http://vel.nz/).  
The focus of the company is on using LaTeX to create high quality documents for business, academic and personal use.  

Creodocs Ltd. consists of: [LaTeXTemplates.com](http://latextemplates.com/) (launched 2012) [LaTeXTypesetting.com](http://latextypesetting.com/) (launched 2013) [Creodocs.com](http://creodocs.com/) (launched 2021)
