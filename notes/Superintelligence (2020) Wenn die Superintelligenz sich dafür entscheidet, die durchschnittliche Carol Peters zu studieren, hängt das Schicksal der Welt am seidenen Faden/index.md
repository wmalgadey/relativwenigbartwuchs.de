While "Superintelligence" is by no accounts a groundbreaking or innovative movie, it should be mentioned that writer Steve Mallory and director Ben Falcone does deliver an entertaining and wholesome story and movie with "Superintelligence".

I was definitely more than adequately entertained by this 2020 movie. The movie has a good and interesting storyline which proved as much fun and enjoyable as it actually does give the audience something to wonder and think about. I liked the concept idea of "Superintelligence" quite a lot actually.

And the storyline also is crammed with some funny moments and they are well delivered and performed by Melissa McCarthy - as this is essentially her movie. But also the voice of James Corden was good as the voice behind the Superintelligence AI. Actually, I have to say that Melissa McCarthy definitely put on one of her more memorable and outstanding performances in "Superintelligence" and she really made this her movie with her performance as Carol.

There was a good pacing to the storyline and writer Steve Mallory definitely was good at constructing a good narrative story that has a very natural and good flow to it. This was something that the movie really benefitted from.

If you enjoy a comedy that has more meaning and depth to it than just provide you with laughs, then "Superintelligence" is definitely well worth taking the time to sit down and watch. I was genuinely entertained by this movie, and I was also nicely surprised with the outcome of the storyline.

My rating of "Superintelligence" lands on a six out of ten stars.
