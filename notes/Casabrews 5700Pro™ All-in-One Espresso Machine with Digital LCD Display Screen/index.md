The 20 bar Italian pressure pump delivers a full-bodied, creamy and caramel espresso extract, giving the water the strength to push through a tightly packed bed of finely ground coffee.

# **Built-in Memory Grinder**

15 adjustable grind settings to control grind level and grind precisely the right amount of coffee. The grind memory remembers your preferred grind amount and uses the exact same grind time for the next brew.

# **PID Temperature Control**

The Thermocoil heating system with PID temperature control provides water at exactly 200°F for perfectly balanced flavors.

# **Powerful 266°F Steam Function**

Enjoy silky, soft micro-foamed milk made in under a minute with powerful steam pressure.

# **Stylish & user friendly design**

With a LCD Display for More Intuitive for selecting steam, hot water and pre-programmed single & double shots, this espresso coffee machine with grinder is very easy to use.Its brushed stainless steel design looks beautiful on any kitchen countertop.
