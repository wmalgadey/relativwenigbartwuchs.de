## Product Description

Modernize your home espresso setup with the Ascaso Steel Uno! A future-facing design update to Ascaso's unique thermoblock platform, the Steel Uno offers accurate PID temperature control, programmable preinfusion, and volumetric shot lengths for effortlessly repeatable espresso. Encased in sleek stainless steel and finished with true walnut furniture, the Ascaso Steel Uno offers shot excellence and accessible steaming in a clean package.

### Powerful Steam

An externally-accessible screw lets you set the max pressure the pump will push through your group head. In addition to giving you an extra parameter to play with, this opens up the Steel Uno to backflushing.

### Electronic Group Temperature Control

A group-integrated thermoblock with electronic PID control adjustable in one-degree increments offers exacting temperature settings.

### Enhanced Volumetric Controls

The Steel UNO's new PID also includes advanced features like temperature and offset control, pre-infusion, power-saving modes, and single or double shot programming.

### Analog Pressure Gauge

The face-mounted pressure gauge offers an easy view to brew pressure over the length of your shot. The vintage industrial look will become the centerpiece of any kitchen

### Excellent Espresso

The Steel Uno brings excellent, easily recreated espresso thanks to its basket options, tight temperature controls, and short water path. The new aluminum & stainless steel thermoblock group assures continuous, unlimited steam with constant pressure and provides enhanced stability and minimal energy consumption.

### Quality Build with Professional Materials

The Steel UNO body is manufactured in powder-coated carbon steel and polished stainless steel. The 58mm professional portafilter features a real walnut wood handle with an ergonomic and aesthetically pleasing design to complement any home kitchen.

### Professional Baskets and Compliments

A classic 58mm portafilter and a bevy of high-quality baskets ensures easy espresso prep with industry-standard accessories.
